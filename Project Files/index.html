Instructions and Policies

Please follow the following instructions carefully for signing up as recipient or a donor

recipient

  • On the sign up button and choose sign up as recipient.
  • Fill in the necessary details. Please double check the registration number you entered corresponds to your hospital to ensure the donation reaches you. Please make sure it is in the format HSMXXXXX. For eg. if your registration number is 12345, write HSM12345.
  • From your account dashboard, please fill up the information of your available items. Next, fill up the “required” portion with the amount of a particular item or the number of staffs you require and click request. You may also edit these values if a change occurs.
  • Click on the donation list to view all the donors who have decided to donate to your hospital. Either of you may contact each other to discuss the method of transportation of the donation that is suitable to you.
  • Once you receive a donation from a particular donor, please click on “confirm donation” next to their name. Your “available” and “required” information would be updated accordingly.

donor

  • on the sign up button and choose sign up as donor.
  • Fill in the necessary details.
  • From your account dashboard, choose the division where you want to donate, then district and then the item you wish to donate.
  • According to your search, a list would appear contacting the information of the hospitals that require the item you want to donate and the quantity required.
  • Select your desired hospital, the quantity of the item you want to donate and click “donate”. Next, kindly contact the hospital to discuss the method of transportation of the donation that is suitable to you.

Please note that we would be keeping a record of your NID which might be used against you if any misconduct or discrepancy is found.
