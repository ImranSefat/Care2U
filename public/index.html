Features

More Than You Think

This site acts as a platform for all of us to be heroes where kindness would be our superpower. At such a difficult time, we should realise that the lives of healthcare workers are much more at risk that that of ours. However, by donating the PPE which you do not require at the moment, you can actually help save a lot of lives. This site acts as a means for you to connect to your nearby hospital that require your donation to continue keeping you and your family safe.

Be the first to know the Covid 19 Updates

All the latest data of Covid-19 patients in Bangladesh are updated here daily after they are shared by the authority, including graphs to give you an idea of the growth pattern of the disease in this country.

Instructions and Policies

Please follow the instructions carefully for signing up as recipient or a donor

recipient

  • Click on the sign up button and choose sign up as recipient.

  • Fill in the necessary details. Please double check the registration number you entered corresponds to your hospital to ensure the donation reaches you. Please make sure it is in the format HSMXXXXX. For eg. if your registration number is 12345, write HSM12345.

  • From your account dashboard, please fill up the information of your available items. Next, fill up the “required” portion with the amount of a particular item you require and click request. You may also edit these values if a change occurs.

  • Below you would find a donation list of all the donors who have decided to donate to your hospital. Either of you may contact each other to discuss the method of transportation of the donation that is suitable to you.

  • Once you receive a donation from a particular donor, please click on “confirm” next to their name. Your “available” and “required” information would be updated accordingly.

donor

  • Click on the sign up button and choose sign up as donor.

  • Fill in the necessary details.

  • From your account dashboard, choose the division, district and area where you want to donate.

  • According to your search, a list would appear containing the information of the hospitals in your specified area that require and the quantity required.

  • Select your desired hospital, the quantity of the item you want to donate and click “donate now”. Next, kindly contact the hospital to discuss the method of transportation of the donation that is suitable to you.

Please note that we would be keeping a record of your NID which might be used against you if any misconduct or discrepancy is found.
